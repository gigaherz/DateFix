DateFix Help:
  {0} [<parameters>] [<working directory>|<filename>]

Parameters:
  -help               Shows this message.
  -recursive          Process subdirectories.
  -date=<date>        Use the specified date instead of current.
  -time=<time>        Use the specified time instead of current.
  -future-only        Process only files with dates > specified.
  -touch-readonly     Temporarily remove the readonly attribute.
  -touch-directories  Process directory dates also, not just files.
